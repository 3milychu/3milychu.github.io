Instructor: Richard The
In this project, the experience is driven by information on all costume objects in the Metropolitan Museum of Art. The UI is designed around revealing patterns around the 7.6k items available, with relevant metadata revealed on hover in all instances and linked to originals on the MET's website. Due to image loading limitations in Chrome and Mozilla, we recommend opening the web app in Safari.

Brunches of Suits Creates a playful setting to explore a subset of costume objects - suits. Metadata on culture is processed and translated into food faces.
