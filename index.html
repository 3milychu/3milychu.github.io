Playing with Hours of Music Played
Northside Festival

Class: Data Aesthetics and Information Visualization
Instructor: Christian Swinehart

This web app explores time and its behaviors as physical symbols.
Data regarding venues, artists and shows listed for Brooklyn's 2017
Northside Festival was scraped by the instructor. I connected the
respective JSON files, parsed by shows per venue, created arrays to store
duration of all shows played per venue, and visualized the result as a
simple rectangle shape which, upon hover, returns the
number of hours played at each venue in number of 360 degree
counter-clockwise spins.
