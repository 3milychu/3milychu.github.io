Cultural Data

Met.erials
Parsons MSDV Major Studio, 2018

Instructor: Richard The
This project analyzed 155k objects containing the Metropolitan Musem of Art's top seven occuring materials. Questions asked: What are representative items made from each material in the MET's collection? What larger patterns are they a part of, and what can we learn from the semiology of cultural artifacts?

Tools Background This project was created in the Major Studio I course within Parson's MS Data Visualization program in partnership with the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York City, NY. We worked with the museum's General Manager of Collection Information to understand the nature of and nuances behind the data - including missing gaps and where the most reliable information was recorded consistently in the dataset.
