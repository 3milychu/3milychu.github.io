The Managerial Landscape

The Managerial Landscape
Interactive Data App, 2017

Did you know there is no statistically significant correlation between female manager representation and female education itself? Surprisingly, it is for every 1% increase in the proportion of post-secondary education going to females, that you see a 0.57 percentage point increase in female manager representation. This interactive web app invites you to explore this correlation. Make it your own.

Launch in Mobile

I advocate transparency, because I do not know what the truth is. I cannot point you to the truth, but I can show you the most interesting relationship I've found and give you an experimental playground where other variables in its stratosphere hang out.
