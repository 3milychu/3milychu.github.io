The Managerial Landscape

The Managerial Landscape
Interactive Data App

Using a dataset from 79 countries procured from the United Nations, I found there is no statistically significant correlation between female manager representation and female education itself. Surprisingly, what proved statistically significant was the Education difference and female manager representation. For every 1% increase in the proportion of females attaining post-secondary education (compared to males), there is a 0.57 percentage point increase in female manager representation.
