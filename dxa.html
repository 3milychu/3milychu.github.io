Microinteraction design2020-2021

digital experience audit (dxa) is a brand auditing system that media strategists at Interpublic Group's agencies to measure, track and benchmark a brand's digital presence. It incorporates machine learning to score and provide recommendations on over 700 attributes including usability, security, architecture and indexability. Over 3,500 audits have been conducted with over 1,000 users.

To meet the needs of account management and strategists who present the product to external-facing partners, microinteractions were designed to focus attention on key data visualization components and provide seamless transition during loading in the main brand summary dashboard for each client.

To add fun and delight to the daily workflow of media strategists, we added easter eggs such as this exploding star upon favoriting a brand in the dxa system.

An often requested feature is the ability to toggle between light and dark modes of the interface. This microinteraction was designed to provide a seamless transition between either display mode.
