Positive Illustration

Your Role in Change
Health & Well-Being Lifestyle App

Script writer: Tatiana Linardopoulu
Illustrator, Motion Designer, Producer: Emily Chu

This is an episode created for the Be a Changemaker series in the Contribution & Sustainability path for a health and well-being education app. Episodes are 1-2 minutes and drive engagement in well-being programs. Content was developed to encourage civic participation. Design and production direction aimed to kindly provoke questions about the roles we play in society, our perception of others and how to best collaborate towards a sustainable world.
