Your Role in Change
Health & Well-Being Lifestyle App, 2017

Researcher, Script writer: Tatiana Linardopoulu Illustrator, Motion Designer, Producer: Emily Chu This is the first episode created for the Be a Changemaker series in the Contribution & Sustainability path for a health and well-being education app. Episodes are 1-2 minutes and drive engagement in well-being programs. Content was developed to encourage civic participation. Design and production direction aimed to prompt questions about the roles we play in society, our perception of others and how to best collaborate towards a sustainable world.

Perspective is Everything
Health & Well-Being Lifestyle App, 2017

Researcher, Script writer: Tatiana Linardopoulu Illustrator, Motion Designer, Producer: Emily Chu This is the second episode created for the Be a Changemaker series in the Contribution & Sustainability path for a health and well-being education app. Content was developed to encourage reflection and expansion of perspective. Design and production direction aimed to relax critical judgment on ourselves by looking at familiar situations through the eyes of a platypus.
