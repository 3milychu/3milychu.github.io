Refreshed Research

The Stanford Marshmellow Experiment
Health & Well-Being Lifestyle App, 2017

Researcher, Script writer: Tatiana Linardopoulu Illustrator, Motion Designer, Producer: Emily Chu This is an episode created for the Savings 101 series in the Money & Contribution path for a health and well-being education app. Episodes are 1-2 minutes and drive engagement in well-being programs. Content was developed to communicate the applications of instant and delayed gratification. Design and production direction aimed to refresh audience of major implications in this landmark research study.
