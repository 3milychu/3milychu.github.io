Project Objective

The goal is to provide a clear and engaging animation to introduce participants to a mobile course on reducing risk for diabetes. Distance learning considerations were very important. Participants must understand how the mobile course is designed and how to progress through each module, including setting up a wireless scale that would be included with the course.
