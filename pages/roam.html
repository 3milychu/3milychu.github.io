Augmenting Intuition & Play

ROAM
Parsons/Verizon A.I. Jam 2017

Videography: Rik Ghosh Voiceover, Motion Graphics: Emily Chu Actors: Quixote Vassilakis, Salman Patwary, Emily Chu ROAM is an artificial intelligence ring-operated adventure maker. This video was built in 36 hours to demonstrate use cases for ROAM, an AI ring that helps you complete your goals through a warm and cool indicator band. We wanted to affirm using AI as extensions of natural human capabilities - in this case - intuition.
