Emily Chu (3milychu)

Interactive & Motion Designer @ Aduro Part-time Faculty @ Parsons Emily Chu designs interactive experiences using data visualization, machine learning and motion. She enjoys arranging and rearranging the aesthetics of information to be embodied, playful and beautiful. She hopes to learn more about the world through her practice, and hopes her findings can delight others in the process. @mylifeasanoodle
