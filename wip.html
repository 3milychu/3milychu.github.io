NuCook
Stanford Well-Being Design Challenge, 2018

The aim of the challenge is to promote healthy behaviors throughout the life span through design. Our team focused on aiding the ability to cook nutritious foods according to individuals' dietary requirements. To support individuals across the lifespan, NuCook records frequently used recipes and personalizes them according to evolving taste and/or nutritional needs.
