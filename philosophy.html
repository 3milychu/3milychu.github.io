Style

"Visual Data"
Storytelling Perspective

When there is a story to be told with data, I start with representation. I play with how variables in the dataset are normally represented and play with other ways it could be represented. I attempt to simplify as much as possible. From there, I play with how behaviors can reiterate the message being communicated.

Rhythm
Production Acumen

I often cheat my way through tight production deadlines thanks to a sense of what needs to happen when. I am able to put things in motion so they coalesce at the right moment. When very little information is available and there is across-the-board shallow understanding of what's even going on, I do possess an inner metronome that guides my production process. It is this acumen I thank for creating something out of nothing.

Multiplying
Design Weakness

I have a soft spot for multiplying layers and colors. Many colors I find in my color palette are products of this multiplication process. The process is both gravitational and self-filtering. Once I find myself going down a rabbit hole, I am very likely to decide on a direction soon after.
