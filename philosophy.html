"Visual Data": On Punny Data
Storytelling Perspective

When there is a story to be told with data, I start with representation. I play with how variables in the dataset are normally represented and play with other ways it could be represented. Piggyback on learned behaviors, but tickle the way it's being used, just 10% differently. I attempt to simplify as much as possible. From there, I play with how behaviors can reiterate the message being communicated. I call this "visual data" because it delights me when the end result dissolves all computation into a visual scene. I want it to look beautiful, approachable, not doing too much. I design for humans with mortgages, with a desire for lifelong learning and good company to share ideas with.
