redesign of custom demographics insights product for CVS

cvs health hub

Role Senior Product Designer, redesign consult

Team Product Design, Product Innovation @ Kinesso, IPG

Design & Development 2020-2021

CVS Health Hub is a custom client product that our partner agencies offer to help store owners gain insight into the demographics surrounding CVS stores and health hubs. The redesign solved for aligning UX/UI with an updated design system, easier map navigation and filtering, as well as focusing user attention on key demographic insights clearly.

The version of CVS health hub before redesign is pictured here. Made in R Shiny, it was subject to limited design customizations. Product owners wanted to update it to make it more usable for the client, better summarize key insights for store owners, reflect the updated design style in use, and make it more easy to theme across partner agencies.

The redesigned product clarifies which locations are cvs health hubs vs. storefronts, organizes map points into expandable clusters to avoid overlapping points, improves readability of key summary insights, and provides easy theming across partner agencies using just their primary brand color.
