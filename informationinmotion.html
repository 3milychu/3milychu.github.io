Types of Investments
Health & Well-Being Lifestyle App

Script writer: Tatiana Linardopoulu
Illustrator, Motion Designer, Producer: Emily Chu

This is an episode created for the Savings 101 series in the Money & Prosperity path for a health and well-being education app. Episodes are 1-2 minutes and drive engagement in well-being programs. Content was developed based on best practices in distance learning. Design and production direction aimed to reduce complexity into visual scenes.
