Motivators

Freedom
Concerning the Spiritual in Art, Kandinsky

I enjoy entertaining ideas of shapes containing a pure message. I echo Kandinksy's ideas of inherent symbolizations evident in simple shapes repeated throughout nature. Working in any new medium or facing constraints, I also find myself seeking the edge of freedom and security. The tension in tracking that fine line is tediously exhilarating.

Connection
Quixote Vassilakis & Emily Chu

This was a song titled "Is It Just Me?" that I co-wrote with Quixote Vassilakis. Building connection is a strong motivator for me. This song reflects the learnings and searchings in love and connection - from uncertainties to blind faith to trust and acceptance.
