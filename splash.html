Design & Development 2020-2021

Splash is a highly scalable and easy to maintain design system that can be themed quickly across the numerous agencies at IPG that use its martech tools to optimize data-driven media planning, campaign management and creative strategy. It is client-facing friendly and supports data-rich workspaces, offering a splash of personality and delight to agency partners' day-to-day.

Activities conducted during the user research phase to best understand the needs of various agencies, product owners,media planners/strategists, analysts, account management and leadership in a new design system:

• Component- and pattern-specific surveys: Creation of surveys to study attitudes, needs and painpoints in components and patterns to prioritize in items such as the navigation in creating an elevated user experience.
• Presentation of all UI concepts to stakeholders: Rounds of presentations to socialize the progress and work in development on UI concepts to all stakeholders involved for feedback. Included comps of relevant product designs, impact on user flow, mobile vs. desktop experience, themeing, ease of maintenance and development.
• Preferences and usability survey: Survey sent out to all stakeholders following rounds of UI concept presentations to summarize feedback on preferences and perception of usability for each UI concept presented.
• General follow-up conversations: Based on any concerns or feedback heard from stakeholders, follow-up conversations to better understand and clarify how the new design system can resolve existing painpoints and address priority needs were conducted.

Since data drives a large portion of the content of IPG's martech tools, Splash includes a dedicated kit of data visualization components. Configured in Figma, it allows for easy switching between different variants of chart types, single vs. multi-series data, states, styles and color schemes. We designed a presentation-poppin' "vibrant" color scheme as well as a monochromatic color scheme that can easily apply any agency's primary color.

The Splash design system comprehensively details out styles, type, grid, icons and components to be used in any product it is applied to. It consists of variants for each component by state and demonstrates common patterns in context. Where states switch using microinteractions, the animation strategy is detailed, demonstrated and linked.

One of the most critical aspects of this design system redesign was the new thinking behind the navigation for all global martech tools. A streamlined navigation experience allowed us to simplify the number of components needed and create a more elegant user flow that allows users to easily understand where they are and what client/market they are working with in their workspaces at all times.
