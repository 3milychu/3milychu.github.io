Design & Development 2020-2021

Audience Console is a product within the Interpublic Group's portfolio of internal media planning products to create, syndicate and report on audiences for audience sizing and campaign delivery. It supports account managers, media planners, analysts and leadership make data-driven decisions on opportunities and showcase value and impact of the organization's services to clients.

User interviews: Led by an information architect, our team created product-specific user interview assets including interview script and wireframes to test with users. Our project manager scheduled meetings with representative users across agencies and key roles for the interviews. We then conducted and documented rounds of interview rounds with users to craft user personas and uncover priority workflow improvements.

Summarization and presentation of findings: An excerpt of our key findings were summarized and presented to product owners and relevant leadership for each round of user interviews to discuss insights, align on priorities and address future paths forward.
